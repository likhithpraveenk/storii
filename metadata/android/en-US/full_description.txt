Storii is a modern, feature-rich client for [Audiobookshelf](https://github.com/advplyr/audiobookshelf) that focuses on a clean user experience and maintainable architecture.

## Features

- Stream audiobooks with background playback and automatic progress sync
- Download books for offline listening – fully functional download manager with pause/resume
- Multi-user and multi-server support, including OIDC authentication
- Personalized home screen with continue listening, recent series, and more
- Advanced library browsing: filters, sorting, series grouping, and search
- Customizable player: sleep timer, playback speed, chapter navigation, and listening history
- Appearance settings: light/dark/system themes, dynamic colors, pure black mode, custom fonts
- Flexible navigation: reorder bottom tabs, choose startup screen

    Note: Podcast support is under development and not yet available.
